### Game Introduction

"ShootZ" is an action-packed shooting game where players battle against zombies and other creatures. Use a variety of weapons to survive in fast-paced combat, facing increasingly challenging waves of enemies. The game is famous for its intense shooting action and diverse weapon systems.

### Gameplay Strategy

1.  **[ES]** **Weapon Upgrades**: Collect resources to upgrade weapons, improving firepower and efficiency.
2.  **[ES]** **Enemy Priority**: Identify different types of enemies and prioritize defeating the most threatening targets.
3.  **[ES]** **Survival Strategy**: Keep moving and use environmental obstacles to block enemy attacks.
4.  **[ES]** **Resource Management**: Allocate ammunition and health wisely, look for supply items.
5.  **[ES]** **Combo Attacks**: Learn to use combinations of different weapons to handle various situations.

### How to Play

*   **[ES]** **Movement**: Use WASD keys or arrow keys to control character movement.
*   **[ES]** **Aim and Shoot**: Use mouse to aim and click to shoot.
*   **[ES]** **Reload**: Press R key to reload.
*   **[ES]** **Weapon Switch**: Use number keys to switch between different weapons.
*   **[ES]** **Special Skills**: Some versions may include special skills or item usage.
