### Game Introduction

[JA TRANSLATION NEEDED] Super Smash Flash is a legendary fan-made fighting game inspired by the official Super Smash Bros. series. It brings together a diverse roster of characters from various video game and anime franchises into a 2D platform-fighting arena. The game is celebrated for its fast-paced action and capturing the spirit of the original console games in a free-to-play Flash format.
