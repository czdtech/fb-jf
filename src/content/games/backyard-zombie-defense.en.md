### Detailed Game Introduction

1.  Cover long paths and shared lanes for efficiency.
2.  Slow + splash near exits to catch leaks.
3.  Early upgrades on high‑impact towers compound value.
4.  Balance damage types for fast vs. tanky zombies.
5.  Save cash for mid‑wave emergencies when needed.
