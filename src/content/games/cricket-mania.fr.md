### Detailed Game Introduction

Cricket Mania captures fast batting sessions. Read line and length, pick appropriate shots, and time swings to beat field placements and rack up runs under target pressures.

### Gameplay Strategy & Walkthrough

1.  **[FR]** Shot selection: Play with the line—leg glance for pads, cover for off‑side.
2.  **[FR]** Timing window: Swing slightly late for leg‑side, earlier for off‑side drives.
3.  **[FR]** Target focus: Track required run rate; rotate strike when boundaries dry up.
4.  **[FR]** Risk control: Avoid aerials when fielders camp deep.
5.  **[FR]** Power plays: Use gaps aggressively when the field is in.
