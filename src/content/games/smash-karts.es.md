### Game Introduction

"Smash Karts" is a multiplayer online kart racing game where players compete in fast-paced races, collect power-ups, and battle opponents while racing through various tracks in colorful 3D environments. The game combines classic kart racing with combat elements, providing players with exciting and fun multiplayer gaming experience.

### Gameplay Strategy

1.  **[ES]** **Power-up Usage**: Learn to use various power-ups wisely, attacking opponents or defending yourself at appropriate times.
2.  **[ES]** **Track Familiarity**: Familiarize yourself with different track layouts to find optimal routes and shortcuts.
3.  **[ES]** **Driving Skills**: Master turning, drifting, and acceleration techniques to gain competitive advantages.
4.  **[ES]** **Tactical Cooperation**: Coordinate with teammates in team modes to fight against enemy players.
5.  **[ES]** **Timing**: Choose the right moments to use power-ups and launch attacks.

### How to Play

*   **[ES]** **Movement**: Use WASD keys or arrow keys to control kart movement.
*   **[ES]** **Acceleration**: Some versions may have additional acceleration keys.
*   **[ES]** **Use Power-ups**: Typically use spacebar or mouse click to use collected power-ups.
*   **[ES]** **Drift**: Master drifting techniques to maintain speed while turning.
