### Detailed Game Introduction

Ant and block puzzle is a casual shape‑placement challenge. Fit given blocks into a board, complete rows/areas to clear space, and stave off top‑outs with efficient packing.

<!-- i18n:section:tips -->
### Gameplay Strategy & Walkthrough

1.  Reserve corners for L/T shapes.
2.  Leave flexible gaps sized for common small pieces.
3.  Don’t build tall narrow wells without a matching piece.
4.  Prioritize clears that open board center.
5.  Undo early when placements feel forced.

<!-- i18n:section:controls -->
### Controls Guide

- `Drag` to place; `rotate` if the mode supports it.
- `Undo`/`Reset` via UI.
