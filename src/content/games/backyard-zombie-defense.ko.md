### Detailed Game Introduction

Backyard Zombie Defense is a lightweight TD: build and upgrade a mix of single‑target and aoe turrets, slow enemies near exits, and pace economy to keep up with scaling waves.

### Gameplay Strategy & Walkthrough

1.  **[KO]** Cover long paths and shared lanes for efficiency.
2.  **[KO]** Slow + splash near exits to catch leaks.
3.  **[KO]** Early upgrades on high‑impact towers compound value.
4.  **[KO]** Balance damage types for fast vs. tanky zombies.
5.  **[KO]** Save cash for mid‑wave emergencies when needed.

### Controls Guide

- Place/Upgrade/Sell via UI; start waves when ready.
