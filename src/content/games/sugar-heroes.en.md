### Gameplay Strategy & Walkthrough

1.  Focus goals: Clear blockers or collect items before pure scoring.
2.  Bottom cascades: Work low for free chain clears.
3.  Save merges: Detonate strong combos at choke points.
4.  Side control: Don’t strand colors on edges.
5.  Two‑ahead planning: Map the next move to preserve setups.
