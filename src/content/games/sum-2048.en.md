### Detailed Game Introduction

Sum 2048 is a relaxed numeric puzzler where you connect equal tiles to merge them into higher values. Good board management, forward planning, and cascade setups produce long runs and big totals.

<!-- i18n:section:tips -->

<!-- i18n:section:how-to-play -->
### Gameplay Strategy & Walkthrough

1.  Centralize highs: Keep larger values toward the middle to allow multi‑direction merges.
2.  Avoid dead corners: Corners trap values; use edges for feeders.
3.  Two‑step plans: Prepare pairs so a merge unlocks another merge.
4.  Space first: When crowded, clear a lane before chasing big merges.
5.  Patience: Incremental merges keep options open longer than rush plays.
