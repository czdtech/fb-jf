### Gameplay Strategy & Walkthrough

1.  Center first: On 3×3, center maximizes forks; corners second.
2.  Block immediately: Always respond to a two‑in‑a‑row.
3.  Create forks: Set up positions where two lines can win next.
4.  Anti‑fork: Play to break opponent fork patterns.
5.  Variants: On larger boards, prioritize flexible lines over early corners.
