### Detailed Game Introduction

Explore a vintage shop full of curios: find hidden objects, match silhouettes, and solve simple locks to open new shelves. Gentle timers and hints make it accessible while still rewarding careful observation.

<!-- i18n:section:tips -->

<!-- i18n:section:how-to-play -->
### Gameplay Strategy & Walkthrough

1.  Grid sweep: Divide scenes and scan zone by zone.
2.  Edge focus: Borders and shadow lines hide items frequently.
3.  Silhouette match: Align outlines with candidate objects before clicking.
4.  Hint rationing: Save hints for the last 1–2 stubborn items.
5.  Contrast tricks: Squint/zoom to spot small contrast differences.
