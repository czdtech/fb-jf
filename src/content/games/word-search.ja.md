### Game Introduction

[JA TRANSLATION NEEDED] "Word Search" is a classic puzzle game where players find hidden words in a grid of letters, searching horizontally, vertically, and diagonally to complete themed word lists. This is a relaxing brain exercise game that helps improve vocabulary and observation skills.

### Gameplay Strategy

[JA TRANSLATION NEEDED] 1.  **Systematic Scanning**: Scan the grid systematically by rows or columns, don't jump around randomly.
[JA TRANSLATION NEEDED] 2.  **Multi-directional Thinking**: Remember words can appear in 8 different directions (horizontal, vertical, diagonal).
[JA TRANSLATION NEEDED] 3.  **Find Long Words First**: Long words are usually easier to spot; finding them first can narrow the search area.
[JA TRANSLATION NEEDED] 4.  **Marking Strategy**: Mark words immediately after finding them to avoid repeated searches.
[JA TRANSLATION NEEDED] 5.  **Theme Association**: Use theme hints to predict what types of words might appear.

*   **Select Words**: Use mouse drag or click to select found words.
