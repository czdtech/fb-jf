### Game Introduction

"Slope 2" is the sequel to the popular endless running game where players control a ball rolling down a steep slope, avoiding obstacles and red blocks while trying to survive as long as possible in this fast-paced challenge. The game continues the core gameplay of the original while adding new features and more challenging level designs.

### Gameplay Strategy

1.  **[DE]** **Reaction Speed**: Practice quick reactions to handle suddenly appearing obstacles and direction changes.
2.  **[DE]** **Path Planning**: Observe obstacle layouts ahead and choose the safest paths.
3.  **[DE]** **Speed Control**: While you can't directly control speed, you can influence the ball's movement through path selection.
4.  **[DE]** **Obstacle Recognition**: Quickly identify different types of obstacles, understanding which can be touched and which must be avoided.
5.  **[DE]** **Focus Training**: Maintain high concentration to avoid mistakes due to distraction.

### How to Play

*   **[DE]** **Left/Right Control**: Use A/D keys or left/right arrow keys to control the ball's left and right movement.
*   **[DE]** **Maintain Balance**: Avoid excessive steering that causes the ball to lose control.
*   **[DE]** **Jump**: Some versions may support jumping functionality (if available).
