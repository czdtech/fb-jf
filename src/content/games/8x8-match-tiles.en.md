### Detailed Game Introduction

8x8 Match Tiles is a tidy pairing puzzle on an 8×8 grid. Match identical tiles under move or time constraints, manage board space, and avoid trapping unmatchable singles.

### Gameplay Strategy & Walkthrough

1.  Open lanes: Keep paths clear to expose deeper tiles.
2.  Prioritize scarce tiles before they clog the board.
3.  Chain clears from the bottom to trigger reveals.
4.  Avoid isolating singles near corners.
5.  Use hints sparingly if available.

### Controls Guide

- Select pairs via click/tap following mode rules.
- Undo/Reset if included.
