### Detailed Game Introduction

Fruit Slot Machine emulates a simple three‑reel slot with classic fruit symbols and occasional bonus features. Each spin is independent; payouts follow the paytable and active paylines. Treat it as light entertainment rather than a strategy game.

### Gameplay Strategy & Walkthrough

1.  **[FR]** Budget first: Set a session budget; don’t chase losses.
2.  **[FR]** Paytable: Learn which symbols and lines pay most; features trigger rarely.
3.  **[FR]** Bet sizing: Use modest, consistent bets to extend playtime.
4.  **[FR]** Breaks: Take short breaks to avoid impulse overbets.
5.  **[FR]** Stop rules: Quit on reaching a win target or budget floor.
