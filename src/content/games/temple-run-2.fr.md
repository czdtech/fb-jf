### Game Introduction

"Temple Run 2" is an endless running adventure game where players navigate treacherous temple paths, collecting coins and power-ups while avoiding obstacles and the pursuing demon monkeys. The game unfolds in stunning environments, offering richer gameplay experience and visual effects than its predecessor.

### Gameplay Strategy

1.  **[FR]** **Collect Coins**: Prioritize collecting coins on the path to upgrade characters and purchase power-ups.
2.  **[FR]** **Power-up Usage**: Use shields, magnets, speed boosts, and other power-ups wisely to improve survival.
3.  **[FR]** **Obstacle Prediction**: Observe obstacles ahead and react timely with jumps, slides, or turns.
4.  **[FR]** **Gem Collection**: Collect special gems to unlock new characters and upgrade abilities.
5.  **[FR]** **Mission Completion**: Complete daily missions to earn extra rewards and experience.

### How to Play

*   **[FR]** **Left/Right Swipe**: Swipe left or right on screen to turn.
*   **[FR]** **Swipe Up to Jump**: Swipe up to jump over obstacles.
*   **[FR]** **Swipe Down to Slide**: Swipe down to slide under low obstacles.
*   **[FR]** **Tilt Control**: Tilt device to collect more coins (in some versions).
