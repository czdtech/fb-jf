### Detailed Game Introduction

Lollipop World is a colorful match‑3 with frosting, wrappers, and chocolate as blockers. Objectives range from score thresholds to collecting candies and clearing jelly. Power‑up merges (bomb+line, color clears) are essential on dense boards.

### Gameplay Strategy & Walkthrough

1.  **[FR]** Objective focus: Clear blockers/collectibles before chasing points.
2.  **[FR]** Bottom play: Work low to create cascading clears.
3.  **[FR]** Combo timing: Save strong merges for choke points.
4.  **[FR]** Wall isolation: Avoid sealing colors along edges.
5.  **[FR]** Move planning: Map 2–3 moves to preserve options.

### Controls Guide

- Drag to swap; tap power‑ups; pause/retry via UI.
