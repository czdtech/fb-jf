### Detailed Game Introduction

Aloha Mahjong offers classic mahjong solitaire. Match open tiles to clear stacked layouts, manage limited shuffles/hints, and solve sets without trapping key pairs.

<!-- i18n:section:tips -->
### Gameplay Strategy & Walkthrough

1.  Open structures: Free tiles that unlock the most others.
2.  Pair priorities: Clear rare symbols early to prevent deadlocks.
3.  Look ahead: Avoid removing tiles that seal deeper pairs.
4.  Use shuffles sparingly when blocked.
5.  Time vs. accuracy: In timed modes, balance speed with safe picks.

<!-- i18n:section:controls -->
### Controls Guide

- Select matching `open` tiles to clear them.
- Hints/Shuffles via UI if available.
