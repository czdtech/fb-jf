### Detailed Game Introduction

Quiz x presents rapid‑fire questions across general knowledge, pictures, and simple logic. Scoring favors speed and correctness; streaks yield bonuses. Broad thinking and elimination beat over‑specialization.

### Gameplay Strategy & Walkthrough

1.  **[ES]** Eliminate: Remove obviously wrong options before committing.
2.  **[ES]** Category scan: Identify topic fast to recall relevant facts.
3.  **[ES]** Streak management: Answer confidently to keep multipliers; skip if unsure (if allowed).
4.  **[ES]** Time buffer: Don’t spend the entire timer on one tough question.
5.  **[ES]** Image prompts: Focus on distinctive features (flags, landmarks, animals).

### Controls Guide
